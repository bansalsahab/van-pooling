# ICP for V1 (Stage 0 Baseline)

## Target Customer

- **Customer type:** B2B employer-operated commute program
- **Region:** India metro tech clusters
- **Company size:** 300-3000 daily commuters
- **Operating pattern:** fixed peak windows (morning entry, evening exit)
- **Fleet model:** owned, leased, or managed vans with dedicated drivers

## Core Operational Problem

- Employee demand is bursty during shift windows.
- Fleet supply is finite and often underutilized outside fixed routes.
- Ops teams need dispatch visibility without manual spreadsheet control.

## Why This ICP First

- Dispatch pressure and pooling tradeoffs are visible early.
- KPI movement (wait time, pickup reliability, seat usage) is measurable in weeks.
- Multi-role workflow (employee, driver, admin) is naturally active every day.

## Primary Buyer and Users

- **Buyer / sponsor:** operations head, transport manager, workplace services lead
- **Daily operator:** dispatch/admin team
- **End users:** employees and drivers

## V1 Success Narrative

- Employees get predictable pickup and live ride clarity.
- Drivers execute assigned trips with fewer manual calls.
- Admins control demand spikes through realtime dispatch and alerts.
- Leadership sees measurable utilization and reliability improvements in-product.
